Format for data entry:

../Data/Exchange/PAIR
Ex:
../Data/Bitfinex/BTCUSD/
    180501-180801.csv
    180801-181101.csv
    ...

timestamp1,timestamp2,open,high,low,close,volume

timestamp 1: The js ts when we started data collection
timestamp 2: The js ts when we stopped collecting data for this candle
